I am currently studying Digital Media Design: 3D Visualisation at University of Winchester. I am finding this to be an excellent course, as it is enabling me to explore how to make incredible art in the digital world, as well as furthering my interest in 3D creation.

I am a conscientious person who works hard and pays attention to detail. I am flexible, quick to pick up new skills and eager to learn from others. My focus is personal development and expansion of work-related skills.
I have always been interested in architecture and interior design. I graduated with a distinction in 2021 from Morley College in London in Art and Design. I also finished my Diploma in Architecture & Interior Design Visualization in 2020 with various software: 3dsmax, AutoCAD and SketchUp.

I am passionate about exterior and interior visualisation. I completed three projects. The first one, a small coffee shop project. Secondly, I visualised a house externally and internally, and last one college class project.
I have experience in producing floor plans.
I enjoy creating and visualising 3D models, particularly modelling lights and animating them with lighting.
My new interest is creating characters, environment, textures, animating, and video editing. I feel very engaged by these processes, and it motivates me to keep progressing.
