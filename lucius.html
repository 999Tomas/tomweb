Planning

1.1 Inspirations

My task for this project was to create assets in our 3D game room.
Our interactive experience in a dark academia aesthetic environment.
I focused on what specific furniture was in this style. Also what characteristic of dark academia aesthetic in materials, patterns, textures, what colours palette.
I found the dark academia aesthetic style required wood, particularly walnut, mahogany, and it elaborately carved. Also that this style works well with rich texture, dark, drab colours palette with forest and olive green.
Next, I focused on how to build our project assets. How many parts of the model, what they call. The most concern was piano asset. It is a complex instrument, has many parts, which was unclear to me. Also what shape they are. This research helped know all it. It answered all questions which I was concerned, and then the next stages went smoothly.
